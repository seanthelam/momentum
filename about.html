About Momentum

Built by physics students, for physics students‚Äîa resource hub for navigating research opportunities and academic pathways at Colorado College.

Our Mission

Momentum is a student-run initiative by the Colorado College Society of Physics Students (SPS) chapter. We recognized that finding and applying to summer research programs can feel overwhelming, and information is often scattered across dozens of websites, departmental emails, and word-of-mouth advice.

Our goal is simple: centralize the best resources, opportunities, and guidance in one place to make the application process more accessible and less stressful for all CC physics students‚Äîfrom freshmen exploring the major to seniors applying to graduate programs.

This is a living document, maintained collaboratively and updated regularly by students who have been through the process themselves. We're committed to supporting our peers through every step of their physics journey.

About CC SPS

Society of Physics Students

The Society of Physics Students (SPS) is a national organization for undergraduate physics enthusiasts. The Colorado College chapter brings together students passionate about physics‚Äîfrom majors to minors to anyone curious about the universe.

What We Do

  • Guest Speakers: Host talks from faculty, industry professionals, and graduate students
  • Social Events: Stargazing nights, physics movie screenings, game nights, and more
  • Professional Development: Workshops on REU applications, grad school, career paths
  • Community Building: Create a supportive network of peers at all levels
  • Outreach: Organize physics demos and activities for local schools
  • Study Groups: Support each other through challenging courses

Join Us!
